I am the President of the Mind Matters Club at St. Thomas. The club works to decrease the stigma surrounding mental health and spread awareness of the resources available. We're hoping to have at least two events this semester!

 
 

I have worked at Fresh French Fries at the Minnesota State Fair for the last six years. It is my favorite job by far. I love the people and the fast-paced environment. The other workers have become another family for me. This year was my first fair as a manager for the booth. With record breaking business, I stayed very busy. I already can't wait for next year!
